D1E1 - Introductions / Show & Tell

In the first session, we introduced ourselves and shared personal projects that reflect who we are. This experience showcased our unique journeys and passions, highlighting the creativity and dedication each of us brings to our work. My first project recreates famous paintings without their bodies to emphasize the transient nature of the human body. My second project illustrates early human history.


D1E2 - Examples of Human-Machine Interactions

In the second session, we were asked to present three examples of human-machine interaction. One example focused on how humans influence machines, another on how machines influence humans, and the third on a balanced interaction where the influence is equal between humans and machines.
